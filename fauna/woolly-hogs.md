## Overview
Woolly Hogs are a group of swine native to [Tseireph](locations/tseireph.md) and the southwestern continent, domesticated by the [Sarvarans](groups/morellic/sarvara*.md). They are known for their distinctive woolly coats, which are often harvested and turned into yarn or thread.
