## Overview
Karshana is a continent of [Qherelh](locations/qherelh.md) in the [2nd realm](locations/2nd-realm*.md). It is to the south-west of [tseireph](locations/tseireph.md), and is the home of groups such as the [Aqhoran](factions/aqhor.md) minikin.
