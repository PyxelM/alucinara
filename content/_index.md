'If Only You Set Me Free' is a book and worldbuilding project by Mylo / Anastasia Hodges, which focusses on themes of existentialism, theology, anthropology, and history. It was concieved in November 2021 during a creative writing mock GCSE test, and has been continually worked on ever since.
