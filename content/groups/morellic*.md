## Overview
The Morellic minikin are an ethnic group tracing its origins to a group of neolithic [minikin](fauna/minikin.md) inhabitants of southern [Tseireph](locations/tseireph.md). They primarily worship the [Petrified Child](cosmology/deities/the-petrified-child.md) and venerate the Morellic prophets, but also exhibit some traits of polytheism.
## History
### Neolithic Era - 'The Mora'
The Morellic minikin orginate in the Mora tribe, which inhabited the steppes of southern Tseireph during the neolithic period. They were pastoral nomads, herding [snoutgoats](private/fauna-old/yurikhs.md) and [woolly hogs](private/fauna-old/woolly-hogs.md) across the plains.
