## Overview
Dark Owls are an intelligent species of owl, related to [widrens](fauna/widrens.md), native to the -1st realm. They appear anthropoid, with silky, night-black feathers covering their bodies. They are carnivores, consuming a diet of small rodents, reptiles, and other animals. They communicate through a language of chirps and calls, and live in civilised, familial communities or hunting groups.
