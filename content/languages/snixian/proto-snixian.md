Proto-Snixian `Proto-Snixian: /t͡ʃé:sòn épā:ɫìç/` was spoken in 8,500YA by the Proto-Snixian [Minikin](fauna/2nd-realm/mammalia/minikin/minikin.md). It acted as the progenitor of the Snixian Languages.
