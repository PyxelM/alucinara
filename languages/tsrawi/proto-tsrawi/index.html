Proto-Tsrawi Proto-Tsrawi: /t͡sʼr̥äʋi pʼinä r̥in͡tä xu/1 was spoken in 10,000YA by a Minikin population in the modern-day Morellic Mountains. They are one of the first known communities to have existed.
